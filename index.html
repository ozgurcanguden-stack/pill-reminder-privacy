Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Pill Reminder – Medication Alarm respects your privacy. This application is designed to work without collecting or storing any personal data.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information, health data, location data, device identifiers, or usage analytics.

Permissions

The app may request permission for notifications and alarms. These permissions are used only to remind you to take your medication.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not specifically designed for children under the age of 13. No personal data is knowingly collected from children.

Internet Connection

The app works offline and does not require an internet connection.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
